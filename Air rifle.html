The Little England

The objective of the course is to provide awareness and knowledge about the sport shooting to civilians. The course consist of 6 classes each session for 2 hrs duration. The course teach about the safe handling of shooting equipment and taking care of the sports equipment. It is also an introductory step to learn about various techniques of shooting, rules to be obeyed for safe gun handling, etc. This course has been made mandatory by the Commissioner of Police, Bangalore, for procurement of Arms License. On the completion of the course a certificate is given to students.
