Surabhi Grand

Hotel Surabhi Grand lodging and banquet hall is a great place to dine in for families. It is located 5km away from Kotilingeshwara Temple and has a decent crowd. The hotel also has an AC dine-in area and ample parking space. The food served here is delicious, with a variety of North Indian starters and South Indian dishes. The tea served here is especially noteworthy, with a cost of just 10 rupees. The service here is also satisfactory. The price for the food is reasonable, making it a great option for those travelling from Bangarpet towards Kotilingeshwara and Vkota. All in all, Hotel Surabhi Grand lodging and banquet hall is a great option for those looking for a good family dining experience.
